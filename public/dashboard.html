step-by-step setup

Requires a TradingView Pro+ plan (webhooks are paid-only). ~1 min setup.

  1. Open a chart with your strategy attached.
  2. Press Alt+A or click the alert (clock) icon. The Create Alert dialog opens.
  3. In Settings: Condition = your strategy, with Order fills only. Expiration = Open-ended.
  4. Paste the Message field above into the Message box.
  5. Switch to Notifications. Check Webhook URL and paste the URL above.
  6. Replace <YOUR_WEBHOOK_SECRET> with the WEBHOOK_SECRET value from your .env.
  7. Click Create. Signals will land in the Activity log on the right.
